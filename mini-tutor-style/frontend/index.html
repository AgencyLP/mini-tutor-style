Concept 01

What is a Carbon Credit?

Goal: understand what one credit lets a company do.

Short explanation

A carbon credit is a permit. Each credit gives a company the right to emit exactly one tonne of carbon dioxide. No credit, no permission.

Strong example

A factory produces 10 tonnes of CO₂ per year. It only holds 8 credits. It must purchase 2 more on the open market — or face a financial penalty.

Memory hook

1 credit = permission for 1 tonne.
